About me

From Paris to Hong Kong all the way to London and Bali, Kim Sane is a DJ/Producer who divides his time crafting house and techno and playing between Europe and Asia. Throughout the years, he offers his own take on Techno and Tech House and is constantly trying to break the border, mixing up the groovy, dirty Deep House with powerful Techno beats.
